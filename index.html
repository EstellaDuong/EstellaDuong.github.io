Estella Duong

Illustrator, Designer, Developer

The first time I burnt my fingerprints off was at the ripe age of 6. My hot glue gun and my bare fingers quickly became close friends after that. Whether it was building miniatures, crafting my own inventions, or experimenting with mixed media drawings, I was always doing something creative. Over a decade later, not much has changed. I’m still burning my fingers off with hot glue, this time while making my own automatic egg incubator, competitive board game, or whatever creative endeavor my heart desires. If I'm not doing that, you'll definitely find me creating digital illustrations, boothing at conventions, or working on my art business!
