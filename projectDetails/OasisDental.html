Summary

I was hired by Oasis Dental to design a fun character to add to the front window of their dental office. The final graphic was created in Adobe Illustrator and features a happy tooth character who is surfing on a toothbrush. You can find this piece of art at Oasis Dental's Aurora, CO location.

The Process

This design went through many iterations as I aimed to capture a fun, eye-catching image that would appeal to both child and adult Oasis Dental patients. At the same time, I also focused on incorporating aspects of their existing minimalistic branding so that the graphic did not look out of place.

After coming up with our idea, I created some initial sketches to discuss with the client. Here, we agreed to simplify the style and decided to iterate further on design 6.

I created some more sketch examples to help visualize how far we wanted to go in terms of simplification. There was a fine balance we had to find between being too detailed and too minimalistic. It was also important to see examples of how the design looked next to the rest of the window text.

In the final stretch of the design process, we agreed on the character's expression and pose, leaving the coloring for last. In an attempt to follow the simple, flowing lines seen in their other signs, I created several versions of coloring that highlighted that. Ultimately, we agreed that bolder fill colors and less focus on lines looked the best. With the design completed, it was ready to be sent off to be printed and displayed at Oasis Dental's office!
