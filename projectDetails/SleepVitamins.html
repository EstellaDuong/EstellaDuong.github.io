Summary

For this design project, I was tasked with creating a packaging solution for a product of my choice. This was for my Text class, where I learned about graphic design, typography, and layout within digital and analogue media. The goal was to develop a visually appealing and functional package that would stand out on a shelf and effectively communicate the product's benefits. In particular, I focused on an engaging color scheme and typography that highlights the most important information. The packaging was designed in Adobe Illustrator and 3D mockups were made in Adobe 3D Substance Stager.
